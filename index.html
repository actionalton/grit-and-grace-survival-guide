The Grit & Grace Survival Guide

How to Keep Going When You're Burned Out, Broke, and Feeling Forgotten

My Story Snapshot

Burnout hits hard — mentally, spiritually, emotionally. I’ve been there. In the trenches of addiction, prison walls, sleepless nights, unpaid bills, and broken communication at home. You’re running on fumes, barely holding it together, praying for a sign that you ain’t invisible. This is that sign.

Name the Lies

  • "God’s not listening."
  • "I’m failing as a parent, partner, provider."
  • "Nothing’s changing — I should just give up."

Speak the Truth

God has not forgotten you. Isaiah 49:15-16 says He’s written your name on His hands. Psalm 34:18 says He’s close to the brokenhearted. That means YOU. Right now.

3-Day Survival Reset

Day 1: No junk food. Pray out loud. Stretch or walk 10 min. Read Psalm 13. Speak truth: “I’m not alone.”
Day 2: Eat protein early. Call someone. Read Romans 8. Move your body. Remind yourself: “God’s working even when I don’t see it.”
Day 3: Journal 3 things you're still grateful for. Get some sunlight. Read Isaiah 40. Say: “I’m in a fight, not a failure.”

Prayer for the Dark Days

God, I feel forgotten. I’m tired of pretending I’m okay. I need You to hold me together. Remind me You’re still with me, even here. I don’t need perfect — I just need real. You know my pain. You’ve seen my tears. Strengthen me for one more day. In Jesus’ name. Amen.

Affirmations to Fight Back

  • God’s not done with me.
  • I can survive today — with Him.
  • I’m not crazy — I’m under attack.
  • Hope still breathes in my story.
  • My struggle is real, but so is God’s grace.
  • My weakness is where His strength shows up.
  • I will not quit. I will rise again.

Closing Charge

You’re not weak. You’re in a battle. This ain't the end of your story. Don’t let the silence convince you that God’s not moving. He is. I’ve walked that fire and I’m telling you — don’t quit. Hold the line. Grit and grace will carry you through.
